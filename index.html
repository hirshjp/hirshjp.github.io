About Us

Hunta Lettings is a property management company set up by landlords to provide exceptional service for other landlords. Our aim is to provide a professional, comprehensive and efficient service at a competitive price.

We are based in the north of England and manage properties across the country including Manchester, Leeds, London and the North East.

At Hunta Lettings, providing an efficient and professional service to our clients is at the core of what we do. Finding the most suitable tenants and looking after your asset is what we aim to achieve.

Whether you are an experienced landlord or a first time investor, we can help you maximise your investment.

Expat Service

We are landlords. We have been expats. We know what we want and we want you to have it too!

We have over 15 years experience in the property market and when it comes to having to find the right people to entrust the management of your property to, we have experienced the good, the bad and the ugly. It may have started from personal experience, but Hunta Lettings has grown from our ability to truly understand our customers and offer a superior solution to expatriate property management needs.

You may be on the other side of the world and feel weighted with the responsibility to your property and tenants. We are here, not only to relieve your burden, but share your responsibility and give you peace of mind to live and work comfortably whilst abroad. We will help find tenants, maintain your property, manage and coordinate repairs, billing, contracts and act on your behalf with tenant management.

Letting Fees Information for Tenants

BEFORE YOU MOVE IN:
Tenant Set up fee: £150 (inc VAT) per property
Referencing one tenant (identity, immigration and visa confirmation, financial credit checks, obtaining references from current or previous employers / landlords and any other relevant information to assess affordability) as well as contract negotiation (amending and agreeing terms) and arranging the tenancy and agreement
Guarantor Fee: £50 (inc VAT) per guarantor (if required)
Covering credit referencing and preparing a Deed of Guarantee as part of the Tenancy Agreement.

AT THE END OF YOUR TENANCY:
Future Landlord Reference Fee: £10 (inc VAT) per reference request
Collating information and preparing a reference for a future landlord or letting agent

UNPAID RENT / RETURNED PAYMENTS
Interest at 8% above Bank of England Base Rate from date due plus an administration charge of £25
