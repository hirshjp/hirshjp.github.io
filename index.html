About Us

Hunta Lettings is a property management company set up by landlords to provide exceptional service for other landlords.

Whether you are an experienced landlord or new to the industry, we can help you maximise your investment!

We are based in the north of England but manage properties across England, including Manchester, Liverpool, Leeds and London.

At Hunta Lettings, providing an efficient and professional service to our clients is at the core of what we do. Finding the most suitable tenants, quickly, is testament to the quality of service we provide. We are committed to providing the best possible customer service and will always be on-hand to help.

It is our responsibility to make our clients feel happy and secure that their asset is in safe hands. This is at the very heart of what we are trying to achieve and it is carried out with passion, dedication and professionalism.
